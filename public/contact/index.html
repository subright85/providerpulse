IsLLMDown

Component-level health for the LLM APIs your stack depends on.

Contact

The fastest way to reach the maintainers of IsLLMDown is through the project's GitHub repository. Most questions and reports are public and benefit from being archived in the open.

Bug reports and feature requests

If you have noticed incorrect data, a broken provider integration, a UI bug, or have an idea for a new feature, please open an issue at github.com/subright85/IsLLMDown/issues.

When reporting a data issue, please include the provider name, the component, the time window in question, and what you expected to see versus what the dashboard shows. That is enough to reproduce most problems.

Questions and discussion

For methodology questions, general feedback, or comparison with other status trackers, the GitHub Discussions board is the right place: github.com/subright85/IsLLMDown/discussions.

Adding a new provider

If you build with a provider that publishes a StatusPage.io endpoint and you would like IsLLMDown to track it, open a Discussions thread with the provider's StatusPage URL. Adding a provider is generally a matter of configuration; we are happy to extend coverage as long as the data source is public, stable, and on StatusPage.io.

Privacy or legal concerns

For privacy questions, see the privacy policy first — most common questions are answered there. For other legal matters, file an issue and tag it legal; we will respond there.

Response time

The project is maintained on a best-effort basis. Most reports get a response within a few days. Critical data-quality issues are prioritised over feature requests.
